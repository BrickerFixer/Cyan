Cyan Crawler

An ethical, user-focused crawler for the Index Metasearch project.

Hello! If you've found this page, you've likely seen "Cyan" in your server logs. Cyan is the web crawler for my open-source search project, Index. Our mission is to build a search experience that is ethical, transparent, and respects users by providing direct access to quality information—without a barrage of ads.

Cyan's purpose is to index the text content of your page so that people can find your valuable website through a more privacy-respecting search tool that will, likely, not bury your website in the search results. (we value small and actually useful resources, you're amazing!)

If you were spammed by the crawler:

That is truly unfortunate, and I sincerely apologize. As a new project, I am constantly refining the crawling behavior to be as polite as possible. If Cyan was overly aggressive, it was unintentional.

  • Please feel free to contact me via email.
  • Or, if you have a GitHub account, you can report an issue.

If you wish to opt-out:

I'd be sad to see you go, but I completely respect your choice. The best way to block Cyan (and other well-behaved bots) is by using a `robots.txt` file. However, if you'd prefer a manual removal, please contact me through the same channels, and I will gladly add your site to my exclusion list.

Interested in learning more?

  • See the project's old structure on GitHub: BrickerFixer/Index.
  • Explore the new, node-based structure: BrickerFixer/Search-node-example.
  • Check out my personal blog at brickerfixer.github.io.

A future, more advanced crawler named "Blu" is planned for larger-scale indexing. It will be built with the same principles of politeness and respect for webmasters.

Thank you for your understanding. I am committed to resolving any issues collaboratively and respectfully.
